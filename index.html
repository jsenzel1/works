    Pet Rock, 2023



    • Pet Rock is a Eurorack synthesizer module that I created which generates one rhythm every 24 hours, and follows real moon cycles to produce changing rhythmic algorithms over the course of many months. All hardware/software/design is all created by me and is open source, and the module is sold at cost of production as an experiment in the incredibly expensive landscape of Eurorack synthesizers
